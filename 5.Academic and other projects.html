Undergraduate lab projects

Real-time human detection, tracking with geographical localization

in flood-affected regions using drone

Level 4, Term 2

Supervised by Dr. Celia Shahnaz

Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology

We built a quadcopter drone to detect and locate humans from above in flooded regions using a fine-tuned YOLO. We used the arducopter microcontroller as flight controller and sent the video feed to the ground station (a laptop) for running YOLO.

Bangla Calendar Clock 3.0

Level 4, Term 1

Supervised by Dr. Sajid Muhaimin Choudhury

Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology

We implemented a Bangla Calender Clock showing Bangla calendar date and time along with English calendar date and time in Bangla alphabets and numerals. The system also had automatic time synchronization in case of power failure without internet connection. This was an extension of the Bangla Calender Clock 2.0 done by our previous batch, and our department senior Saleh Ahmed Khan provided a lot of help along with the codes of their version. The codes are here.

Facial Expression Classification using Deep Learning

Level 4, Term 1

Supervised by Dr. Shaikh Anowarul Fattah

Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology

We reimplemented the machine learning model from a journal paper title ”Facial Expression Recognition Using Multi-Branch Attention Convolutional Neural Network” by Yinggang He from scratch as there was no code available for it, slightly changed some parameters to gain a slightly higher accuracy. We used Keras and Tensorflow as ML libraries. The codes are here.
